# Mercy Row Campaign

## Campaign Overview

This is an urban fantasy Dragonbane campaign set in the gritty district of Mercy Row, where ancient magic meets modern intrigue.

## Setting

Mercy Row is a district where:
- Old traditions clash with new realities
- Magic is commonplace but regulated
- Political tensions simmer beneath the surface
- Adventure lurks around every corner

## Campaign Themes

- Urban exploration and investigation
- Political intrigue and factional conflict
- The tension between tradition and progress
- Personal relationships in a complex world

## House Rules

- Emphasis on social encounters and investigation
- Magic is visible and regulated in urban areas
- Focus on character relationships and personal stakes
