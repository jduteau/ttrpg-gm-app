# The Secret of the Dragon Emperor


  *** SOLO ADVENTURER HEALING EXCEPTIONS ***
  (1) Stretch Rest — self-healing: may make a HEALING roll to tend own wounds.
      On a success, heal 2D6 HP instead of the standard D6.
  (2) At 0 HP — Rally self: may attempt to rally with NO bane on the
      WIL roll (standard rules impose a bane for self-rally with WIL).
  (3) At 0 HP — Save own life: may make a HEALING roll on themselves; a
      success counts as one of the three death roll successes needed to
      stabilise.
