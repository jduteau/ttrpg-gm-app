## Locations

- **[LOCATIONS]** Dustfall is a small town of approximately 200 souls, situated near alkali flats to the west that turn the color of old bone in late afternoon light [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** Dustfall has a main street, a water tower, a church with a steeple that lists slightly to the south, and cattle pens at the far end (mostly empty) [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** Marta Reyes's livery stable — at the near end of main street — burned to the ground the night before Jake's arrival; the interior is gutted, the stalls collapsed and black [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** Marta's small adobe house sits forty feet back from the stable ruins; a corral out back survived the fire [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** Marta charges $1/day with feed for boarding at the corral [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** Ike Pruitt's ranch is four miles north of Dustfall; a water tower with an old bullet hole eight feet up marks the lane, which goes left off the main road [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** Pruitt's ranch is modest but well-maintained — a weathered house, a barn in honest repair; the cut fence line is visible to the east, approximately two hundred yards of wire gone [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** A saloon exists on main street; several horses were tied outside mid-afternoon [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** A general store exists on main street [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** The Sandoval place has a well that was poisoned roughly three weeks before Jake's arrival [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** A dead surveyor is buried in Pruitt's south pasture [S1]
- **[LOCATIONS]** The alkali flats lie to the west of Dustfall [S1]

- **[FACTIONS]** Garrison Cole's operation — Cole's bank, his foreman Dix, and Dix's crew of at least three men including the Caulder brothers; engaged in a covert land-acquisition campaign on behalf of (or in advance of) the railroad [S1]
